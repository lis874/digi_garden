We as humans form emotional attachments to objects, because they act as an extension of ourselves. They become a manifestation of our emotions, feelings, memories, etc.

However, does this relationship only form between us and physical objects? Or can digital “objects" create this same kind of connection?

In Digi Garden, you’ll find multiple small experiments that explore the idea of emotional connection with digital “objects".

New experiments will be added to the garden periodically.
